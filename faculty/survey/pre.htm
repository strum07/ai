Faculty Tutorial on Promoting Academic Integrity

Pre-Survey

The purpose of this pre-questionnaire is to assess the effectiveness of the online tutorial for faculty on academic integrity developed with the support of a grant from the Committee for the Improvement of Undergraduate Education at NIU. Your participation is voluntary and no personal identification information is collected or disseminated as part of your participation. Only a summary of responses from all participants will be used to analyze the effectiveness of the tutorial.
