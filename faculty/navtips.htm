Navigation Tips (back)

This tutorial can be viewed completely online using a Java-enabled Internet Explorer browser. The tutorial may take approximately an hour to review the content and complete the activities fully, depending on one's background, Web navigation skills, and the type of access to the Web. The tutorial does not record any data about individual users or their quiz scores.

To Begin
To begin the tutorial, click on the "Get Started" button and follow the navigation options provided on the top of the screen or at the bottom of content sections as necessary. Make sure to disable any pop-up blockers before beginning. To navigate the content sections of the tutorial, click on the navigation arrow buttons ( ) and view the content screens. The screens can be resized by moving the mouse cursor over the edge of the screens and clicking and holding the resizing arrows displayed.
Quizzes
A quiz is available after viewing each section of the tutorial. To take a quiz, navigate the quizzes, click on the hyperlinked quiz number to take a particular quiz and then click on the appropriate response and Submit button for each question on a quiz.
Cases
To navigate the cases, click on the hyperlinked case number to view the particular case scenario and explore its various possibilities. <screen capture to be updated>>>
Exit
To exit the tutorial, click on the "Exit Tutorial" button at the end of each section of the tutorial or click on the X button on the top right corner of the main page of the tutorial, and close the Web browser fully.
