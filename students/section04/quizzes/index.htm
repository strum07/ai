Online Tutorial on Academic Integrity
Cheating, Fabrication, Falsification and Sabotage - Quiz

This quiz consists of 10 questions carefully designed to help you self-assess your comprehension of the information presented in this section. Each question in the quiz is of multiple-choice format. Read each question carefully, and click on the button next to your response that is based on the information covered on the topic in the module. Each correct or incorrect response will result in appropriate feedback immediately at the bottom of the screen.

The total score for the quiz is based on your responses to all 10 questions. You can retake an incorrectly answered question again and again and get the correct response, but only your first response for a question will be taken into consideration for the 70% correct responses necessary to obtain the certificate.

If you skip a question or close the quiz window before responding to all the questions then that quiz will not be scored and the View Scores and Print Certificate buttons will not be displayed. If you close the tutorial completely or exit the browser before completing all the quizzes, then the scores cannot be viewed and the certificate cannot be printed as the tutorial does not save any user data in the Web site after the user has closed the tutorial. This Web site does not save any data on users' interactions with the tutorial.

To begin the quiz, click on "Start Quiz" button below or click on the "Return to Tutorials" button to return the tutorials. After responding to all 10 questions, click on "Close" to exit the quiz and return to this window. Then click on "Return to Tutorials" in this window to go back to the topics.

After you have taken the quizzes for all the topics, the tutorial will display "View final score / Get certificate" button. Click on the button for viewing the final scores and printing the certificate of completion.

REMINDER: Do NOT close this window. When you are finished with the quiz, click the "Return to Tutorial" button.
